Exercice 4#

The Exclusive or (XOR) or exclusive disjunction is a logical operation that is true if and only if its arguments differ. Let us consider two booleans value1 and value2. The result of a XOR operation is equal to:

  • 1 if exactly one of the two booleans is equal to 1,

  • 0 in all the other cases.
